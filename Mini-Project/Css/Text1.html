Discovering Kenya: A Tapestry of Natural Wonders and Cultural Riches

Situated on the eastern coast of Africa, Kenya is a captivating mosaic of breathtaking landscapes, diverse wildlife, and vibrant cultures. Renowned for its iconic savannahs that play host to the Great Wildebeest Migration in the Maasai Mara, Kenya boasts an unparalleled array of natural wonders. From the snow-capped peaks of Mount Kenya to the pristine beaches along the Indian Ocean, the country's geography is as diverse as its people.
