Planet's in the Solar System
  1. Mercury: The closest planet to the Sun.
  2. Venus: Known for its thick, toxic atmosphere.
  3. Jupiter: The largest planet, with a prominent Great Red Spot.
  4. Saturn: Famous for its stunning ring system.
  5. Uranus: Tilted on its side, unique among the planets.
  6. Neptune: The farthest known planet from the Sun.
  7. Earth: Our home planet, with a diverse range of life.
  8. Mars: Often called the "Red Planet," known for its rusty color.
